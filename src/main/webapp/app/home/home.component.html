What Can Volunteer Kind Do For You?

In this day and age we all depend on software to make our lives easier. Managing charity events should be no different. Volunteer Kind addresses personnel recruitment and event management making it easier to achieve the goals of any planned event.

Tap into the potential of your entire community with Volunteer Kind's recruitment features which allow volunteers to find you.

Allow volunteers registered within the same event to connect with each other and their organizers to ensure everyone's needs are met, including personnel requirements.

No need to have sleepless nights over data protection. We use Keycloak authentication to handle personnel logins (with OAuth 2.0). Rest assured that we here at Volunteer Kind take your privacy seriously and will never disclose any information unless required by law.

No matter how you wish to implement Volunteer Kind's features, you will surely have a better way to manage your charity event needs and bring your community together in the process.
